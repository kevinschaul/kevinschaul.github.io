New: Researchers found a clever way to reveal AI biases, like the states with the laziest people, or the cities with the best pizza. [Look up your city](https://wapo.st/4bMoWqk)

The researchers systematically asked an LLM to compare two places, for every combination, resulting in the scores we visualized. Check out the [full dataset and paper](inequalities.ai/), by Francisco W. Kerche Matthew Zook and Mark Graham.
