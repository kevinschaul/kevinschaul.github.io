About Kevin

Kevin Schaul is a data journalist with a special interest in cartography and public policy. He is set to graduate from the University of Minnesota with a degree in computer science, though all his professional work has been in newsrooms.

He grew up in the Windy City suburbs of Gurnee and Lake Forest, where he developed a deep love for Chicago sports and deep dish pizza. In his free time, Kevin dabbles in photography and distance running.
